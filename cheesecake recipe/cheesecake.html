Cheesecake recipe!

Description

Some of the key ingredients to start of will be the graham cracker
crust. This will include graham cracker crumbs, melted butter,sugar,
and salt. After this stir the ingredients until well mixed and press
the crumbs on the bottom of the pan into an even layer.

Next will be the actual batter for the Cheesecake. In a bowl combine the following
ingredients cream cheese, sugar, and flour. With an electric mixer beat on medium
speed until smooth for 1 minute. Add vanilla flavoring, lemon juice, lemon zest,
and salt then beat on low speed until combined.

Next are the final steps, add each egg beat on low speed until mixed
into batter. Mix sour cream do not under or over-mix make sure
batter is smooth. Start to pour batter on top of the crust, next set
cheesecake in large roasting pan. Pour hot water in pan until about one inch
up to the cake pan. The steam from the water helps prevent the cheesecake
from drying and cracking. Bake the cake is set it should not look like a liquid
but will wobble it will harden as it cools down. Let cool for 8 hours in your
refrigerator for 8 hours and then enjoy!!

Ingredients

For the crust

  • 11/2 Cups graham cracker crumbs 12 whole crackers
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

For the filling

  • 32 oz or four 8-oz blocks of cream cheese(room temperature)
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon packed lemon zest, 1 lemon
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2cup sour cream
  • equipment: 10-inch Springform pan 18-inch aluminum foil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375℉
  2. Make Graham Cracker Crust
  3. Make Cheesecake Batter
  4. Bake
  5. Cool
  6. Enjoy!
